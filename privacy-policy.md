# Foxy Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

Foxy ("Foxy", "the App", "we", "us", or "our") is a journaling, mood tracking, and self-improvement app created by Sebastian Rubina. This Privacy Policy explains what information Foxy collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

If you have questions, contact us at:

Sebastian Rubina  
sebastianrubinas@gmail.com

## 1. Summary

Foxy is designed around private reflection. Your journal entries, mood ratings, goals, milestones, tags, and custom metrics may include sensitive personal information. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your journal content for advertising.

Foxy uses Firebase to provide account and cloud sync features, Sign in with Apple for account linking, and Apple's StoreKit for subscriptions.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use Foxy, you may choose to create or save:

- Journal entries, titles, body text, dates, tags, and prompts.
- Mood scores and well-being metric ratings.
- Custom metrics you create.
- Weight entries, if you choose to track weight.
- Goals, milestones, progress, and goal reviews.
- Reminder preferences.
- App settings, such as preferred weight unit.
- Account information connected to Sign in with Apple, such as your Apple-provided name if you choose to share it.

### Account and Authentication Information

Foxy may create an anonymous Firebase account so your data can be saved. If you choose to use Sign in with Apple, your account may be linked to your Apple ID through Firebase Authentication.

Depending on your Apple settings, Apple may provide your name and relay email address. Foxy does not receive your Apple ID password.

### Subscription Information

Foxy uses Apple's StoreKit for subscriptions. Apple processes purchases, free trials, renewals, cancellations, refunds, and payment information. Foxy receives subscription entitlement information, such as whether you have an active premium subscription. Foxy does not receive your full payment card details.

### Device and Technical Information

Firebase and Apple services may process technical information needed to run the App, authenticate users, sync data, prevent abuse, diagnose issues, and operate subscriptions. This may include device identifiers, app instance identifiers, logs, crash-related information, authentication tokens, timestamps, and similar technical data.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Save and sync your journal entries, metrics, goals, and settings.
- Show insights, trends, charts, badges, and progress summaries.
- Let you restore access after reinstalling the App or using another device, if your account is linked.
- Provide subscriptions and premium access.
- Schedule local reminders when you enable them.
- Improve reliability, security, and app functionality.
- Respond to support, privacy, or account deletion requests.

## 4. Journal and Mental Health Information

Foxy may store information about your mood, stress, weight, habits, goals, and personal reflections. This information can be sensitive.

Foxy is for journaling, self-reflection, and personal tracking. Foxy is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical or mental health condition.

If you are experiencing a crisis, emergency, or thoughts of self-harm, contact emergency services or a qualified professional immediately.

## 5. Local Notifications

If you enable reminders, Foxy may schedule local notifications on your device. These reminders are generated by your device. Foxy does not need Firebase Cloud Messaging for the current reminder feature.

You can disable notifications in the App or in iOS Settings.

## 6. App Lock and Biometrics

Foxy may allow you to protect the App using Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode. Biometric authentication is handled by iOS. Foxy does not receive or store your face, fingerprint, or biometric data.

## 7. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share or process information through service providers that help operate Foxy, including:

- Firebase / Google services for authentication, Firestore cloud storage, and related infrastructure.
- Apple services for Sign in with Apple, StoreKit subscriptions, App Store distribution, and device-level features.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, to investigate abuse, or to comply with valid legal requests.

## 8. Data Storage and Security

Foxy stores user data in Firebase Cloud Firestore under user-scoped records. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is perfectly secure. You are responsible for keeping access to your device and Apple account secure.

## 9. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active or as needed to provide the App. We may also retain information as required by law, to resolve disputes, enforce agreements, prevent abuse, or maintain security.

If you delete individual journal entries or goals in the App, Foxy attempts to remove those records from Firestore.

## 10. Account Deletion and Data Requests

Foxy includes an account deletion option in Settings. At this stage, the App marks the account as disabled and signs you out. For full deletion requests or privacy requests, contact:

sebastianrubinas@gmail.com

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal information. We will respond to requests as required by applicable law.

## 11. Children

Foxy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

## 12. International Processing

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including where Firebase, Apple, and their service providers operate. Data protection laws may differ from those in your location.

## 13. Your Choices

You can:

- Edit or delete journal entries.
- Edit or delete goals and custom metrics.
- Disable local notifications.
- Turn off App Lock.
- Use Sign in with Apple to help recover and sync your account.
- Manage subscriptions through your Apple ID settings.
- Contact us with privacy or deletion requests.

## 14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will take reasonable steps to notify users, such as updating the policy date or providing notice in the App or App Store listing.
